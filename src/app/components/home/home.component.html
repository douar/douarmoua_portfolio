My Journey

As immigrants my parents were determined to make ends meet with their own effort and skills, so they have always been entrepreneurs. This lead me to pursue a degree in Business & Marketing Education and being the first in my family to achieve higher education. After graduating I became more involved with the family business, but I always felt like I was missing something.

In 2022, I finally took the courage to stray from my family's work to pursue my own interest, leading me to software development. I joined York Solutions intensive Barrier to Entry (B2E) program, learned how to develop web applications, and gained valuable experience in my first dev role at Optum. Today I continue to grow my passion for developing user friendly software applications.
